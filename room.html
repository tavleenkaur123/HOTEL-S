Hotel Shefaf features 248 well-appointed rooms including Suites, Executive suites and Family rooms offering pool or city views. The suites and rooms are fitted with every modern convenience required for a comfortable stay, such as ergonomic work desks, luxurious beds, and elegant baths.

FAMILY SUITE

Experience the utmost convenience for your family of four in our well-designed accommodations. Our spacious suites are thoughtfully crafted to cater to your family's needs, offering ample space and comfort. Whether you're traveling with children or friends, we provide the perfect setting for a delightful stay where everyone can relax, unwind, and create lasting memories together. Our Family Room, a combination of two separate rooms. It includes a spacious king room of 296 square feet (27.5 square meters) and an equally roomy twin room of the same size, offering ample space for your family. This accommodation comes complete with two walk-in wardrobes, two well-appointed washrooms, two functional work desks, Wi-Fi connectivity, and a generously sized bay window that provides scenic views of either the pool area or the bustling cityscape.

EXECUTIVE SUITE

Experience our thoughtfully designed 542 square feet (50.3 square meter) suite, where comfort reigns supreme with a king-size bedroom, a well-appointed bathtub, a convenient work desk, and complimentary wireless internet to keep you connected. Each Executive suite offers sweeping bay windows, granting picturesque views of either the serene pool or the bustling cityscape. Elevated to a high floor, it presents the perfect vantage point to appreciate the skyline and panoramic cityscape. Enjoy added benefits with our Executive suite, including a complimentary breakfast and delightful Happy Hours at our Gold Villa.
